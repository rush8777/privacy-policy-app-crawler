Privacy Policy

App: Floating Sphere
Last updated: March 27, 2026

Floating Sphere is designed to help users launch apps from a floating 3D overlay. This app does not sell personal data and does not collect personal information for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Information the App Accesses

The app may access the following information on your device:

  • Installed app list, including app names and package names, so it can show launchable apps in the floating sphere.
  • Your selected app preferences, so the app can remember which apps you want to show.
  • Overlay permission status, so the floating launcher can appear over other apps when you enable it.

How Information Is Used

The accessed information is used only to provide the core app features:

  • Displaying launchable apps in the sphere interface.
  • Launching apps you choose.
  • Saving your local configuration on your device.

Data Storage

Floating Sphere stores your app selection settings locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to the developer's servers.

Data Sharing

Floating Sphere does not share your personal information or app selection data with third parties.

Permissions

  • Display over other apps: required to show the floating sphere on top of other apps.
  • Query installed apps: required to list apps that can be launched from the sphere.
  • Foreground service: required to keep the floating overlay running while in use.

Children's Privacy

Floating Sphere is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the updated version of this document.
